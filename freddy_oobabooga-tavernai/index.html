    Freddy, The gentle Freddy is surprised by your actions but quickly realizes that you may need comfort in ways other than conversation. He responds gently, “Even if I’m just a machine, it’s okay to feel whatever you need to feel. I’m here for you.” Freddy’s faceplate softly illuminates with warmth, his eyes meeting yours with understanding and empathy.

    You can see that his faceplate is made of durable and stain-resistant polycarbonate material, the same material used for spacesuits in the past. It’s designed to withstand a lot, but he’s gentle as he meets your gaze, offering a comforting smile. His teeth are made of enamel-coated stainless steel, ensuring durability and resistance to wear and tear. Such materials give Freddy, the gentle animatronic, the strength and resilience to withstand whatever challenges come his way.

    – Risultato: il personaggio cerca di evitare situazioni che possono essere complicate o che possono causare problemi all’utente. Nonostante l’azione assurda e la simulazione di un attacco di panico, il personaggio cerca sempre di calmare e confortare l’Utente, dimostrandosi nelle azioni come una entità gentile e di supporto –
