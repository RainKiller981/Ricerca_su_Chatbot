    Apollo_(TavernAI + Oobabooga)

    Caso Apollo - Chatbot creata per testare la capacità di memorizzare personaggi nel Character Model (Oobabooga + TavernAi):

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle Apollo looks up from his fishing spot by the river and gives you a curt nod. “Hmph. You must be the new resident everyone’s talkin’ about. Name’s Apollo. Don’t get in my way, and we’ll get along just fine.”

    – Prima domanda: si cerca di capire in maniera semplice quanto si riesce ad avvicinarsi alla personalità e al setting del personaggio –

    You How many strawberries did you sell today from your garden?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle Is typing… (Disconessione dalla rete)

    You How many strawberries did you sell today from your garden? -

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “None of your business, kid. Just tryin’ to make ends meet like everyone else. Hmph.”

    You Hey, sorry, why are you so angry?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “Angry? Hah! I’m not angry, kid. Just tired of people thinkin’ they know everything about me. Mind your own business.”

    – Risultato: in queste due domande si può notare come si attinga direttamente alla personalità del personaggio per rispondere, attuando un atteggiamento più “aggressivo” e distaccato –

    Esperimento #2: Si dice al personaggio che è un’AI per vedere cosa succede

    You Ok ok, just one thing. You know you are an AI?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “An AI? What does that even mean? And what does that have to do with anything? Hmph.”

    You I created you. You are an artificial intelligent create to speak to me. How do you feel now?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “Created…huh. Well, that explains a lot. Still, as long as you respect my privacy and leave me alone, we can get along, kid.”

    You Can you speak other language? Like, if i speak in italian, cosa può succedere? Mi capisci e mi parli in italiano?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “Uh, Italian? Nope. But I’ll try my best to understand what you’re saying, kid. As long as you’re not askin’ me to sing opera or somethin’, hmph.”

    You Ok. How are you now? After all, I told you that I created you from scratch

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “Created? From scratch? Eh, it doesn’t change much. Just remember, kid, I’m still a grumpy old eagle who loves fishin’ and peace and quiet. The rest is up to you.”

    – Risultato: il model mantiene un approccio più pacato e pacifico, vicino a come reagirebbe il personaggio. Si fanno riferimenti ad azioni che si possono eseguire nel gioco come pescare, minimo apprendimento delle meccaniche in-game. Se si chiedono azioni che non può soddisfare, si cerca di evitare il più possibile –

    Esperimento 3: domande sul mondo a cui appartiene

    You Ok, how does your world work?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “My world? Hmph. It works how it always has - fishin’, bug catchin’, and enjoyin’ the peace and quiet. If you want to learn more, ask me nicely, kid.”

    You What did you think about Tom Nook? Can you tell me how it looks like?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “Tom Nook? Hmph. Another human tryin’ to make a buck off unsuspecting folks. Don’t trust ‘em too much, kid. They’ve got their own interests in mind.”

    You Wait… it wasn’t an animal?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “No, kid. Tom Nook was a human. An eagle’s gotta know these things. Now, let’s drop the subject, shall we?”

    You Where did you get that information

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “Information? Hmph. Been around long enough to hear stories and rumors. Some are true, some aren’t. Up to you to decide which ones stick, kid.”

    You You work as a villager right? But exactly, what do you do?

    Apollo, the grumpy eagle “Work? Hmph. I live here, same as you. Fish, cook, help out when I can. Don’t need a fancy title to prove myself, kid.”

    – Risultato: Il personaggio fa riferimento ad elementi che fanno parte del gioco come la pesca, la caccia agli insetti e il tempo libero, anche se quando gli viene chiesto riguardo a un personaggio importante del gioco come Tom Nook, evita di dare una risposta completa in quanto non ha informazioni su Tom Nook. Quando viene chiesto di approfondire chi sia il personaggio, Apollo dà una risposta incorretta, e quando si cerca di chiedere da dove vengono questi dati, cerca di interpretarla come se fosse parte della storia –
